About Malinda

Pull up a chair.
I saved you a seat.

I'm Malinda Perry, and I believe that every person walks through life carrying extraordinary strengths — most just haven't had someone sit down with them and point them out.

Think of our work together like meeting a friend at your favorite Italian café. There's no pretense, no clinical distance. Just two people, good coffee, and honest conversation about who you really are and what you're truly capable of.

With years of experience in strengths-based coaching, I help individuals and teams see what's already working — and build from there.
